WHO
WE ARE

RZN IS A PLACE WHERE QUALITY IS TOP PRIORITY, WHICH MEANS THAT EVERY ARTIST TAKES THEIR TIME TO GET TO KNOW YOU BEFORE THEY START CUTTING AND TRIMMING.

We listen to your desires and make suggestions based on out years of experience. We always cut to character. We create a look that fits your personality and lifestyle, so you'll be happy with your new cut long after you've left the salon.

RZN STUDIO is an intimate hair salon and barbershop where the fast and ever changing world of fashion meets with the lot key local Amsterdammer vibe. its's a place where expats meet permanent residents, where young creatives as well as families feel at home. Located in the centre of Amsterdam, our group of hair loving professionals would love to create a look that makes you feel at your best.
